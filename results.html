Results

The correlation between the smoothness of controls and the enjoyment while expected, does show that the game at its heart is fun. However, the with an average rating of 3.65 with 0 being very good and 8 being very bad, the over satisfaction with how the controls behaved was slightly better than average. One question the participants were asked is "how would you change the movement speed of the game? 8 being slow it down a lot, 0 speed it up a lot, 4 keep it the same". The average of all the results is 4.46. This response is very easy to take forward into a development, as it shows that most people found the game slightly too fast. No correlation between Past mobile experience and understanding of the controls. This could mean that the controls are difficult to understand even to experts. On the other hand, it could show that the controls are unique.
